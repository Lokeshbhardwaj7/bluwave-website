Who We Are

Leading Manufacturer in Kandla SEZ

BLUWAVE INTERNATIONAL is a premier manufacturing company located in the Kandla Special Economic Zone, Gandhidham, Gujarat. We specialize in producing high-quality food products that meet international standards.

With our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility and commitment to excellence, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the food industry. Our product range includes refined iodized salt, synthetic white vinegar, and IR64 parboiled rice, all manufactured under strict quality control measures.
